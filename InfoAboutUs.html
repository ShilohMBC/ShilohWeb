About Us

It is the desire of our staff and our congregation, that all who visit here will see this site as a spiritual resource to strengthen and aid you in those needs which grip the contemporary family. We especially want this to be a library support for your biblical needs, Christian growth, and daily inquiry.

Shiloh Missionary Baptist Church is a church where everybody is somebody and nobody is nobody; where love is personified, humanity is amplified and God is glorified, worshipped and served. We are a Gospel-driven church with a multicultural congregation who are determined to preach, teach and live the redeeming love of God to a lost, but changing world.

We have a commitment to inclusiveness. All who desire to come and worship with us are welcome and are invited to come and join our church family.

You may simply want to attend our services; welcome! You may only want to visit; welcome! You may want to join by association or watchcare; welcome! Or, you may desire full membership in our church family; whosoever will, come and welcome!

Shiloh Missionary Baptist Church is dually aligned with the American Baptist Churches USA and the National Baptist Convention USA, Inc. Shiloh was organized in 1952 and has been providing ministry in the city of Anchorage for almost 54 years.

We are located in Anchorage, Alaska at the entrance to Woodside Village, at 20th Avenue and the New Seward Highway. Click here or on the Map to Shiloh button on the left menu bar for directions to our church. Thank you and we look forward to meeting you and your family!
